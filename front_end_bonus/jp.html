Japan Sales by Platform

Japanese Sales by Platform Analysis

The Japanese market is dominated by mobile gaming devices such as the Gameboy and Nintendo DS variations, as well as retro gaming platforms that are more than 15 years old such as the original Playstation and the SNES. Playstation consoles perform better than Xbox consoles, with Xbox gathering almost no support in Japan.
